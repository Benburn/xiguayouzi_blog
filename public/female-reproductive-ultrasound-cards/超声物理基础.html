2.1 超声诊断基础

超声成像在过去半个世纪以来一直是发展最快的医学成像方式之一。 诊断超声具有多项优势，包括安全、无创性、便携性、易用性、经济性和提供实时成像的能力。 本节将概述超声技术的基础知识及其在妇科的应用，重点关注女性生殖健康。

2.1.1 超声

超声是指物体产生的机械振动波，属于频率超过 20 kHz 的声波。 在医学中，超声利用其独特的物理特性——特别是超声波在穿过人体组织和器官时的声学特性——用于诊断和治疗目的。

当超声波穿过人体组织时，它们主要表现为具有极高频率和短波长的纵向波，能够在特定方向上传播。随着这些波的传播，它们会发生各种物理现象，包括反射、折射、散射和衍射。这些过程构成了临床超声成像的基础。

2.1 Fundamentals of Ultrasonic Diagnosis

Ultrasound imaging has been one of the fastest-evolving medical imaging modalities over the past half-century. Diagnostic ultrasound offers several advantages, including safety, noninvasiveness, portability, ease of use, affordability, and the ability to provide real-time imaging. This section will outline the fundamentals of ultrasound technology and its applications in gynecology, focusing on female reproductive health.

2.1.1 Ultrasound

Ultrasound refers to mechanical vibration waves generated by an object, classified as sound waves with a frequency exceeding 20 kHz. In medicine, ultrasound leverages its unique physical properties—specifically the acoustic characteristics of ultrasonic waves as they propagate through human tissues and organs—for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes.

When ultrasound waves travel through human tissues, they primarily manifest as longitudinal waves with very high frequencies and short wavelengths, capable of transmitting in a specific direction. As these waves propagate, they undergo various physical phenomena, including reflection, refraction, scattering, and diffraction. These processes form the foundation of clinical ultrasound imaging.
